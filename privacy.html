Privacy Policy

Waystone Clinical Psychology  |  Last updated: March 2026

Waystone Clinical Psychology is operated by Katy Faure, a registered psychologist (AHPRA No. PSY0002394267, ABN: 47 917 643 895). Waystone Clinical Psychology is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles, the Health Records Act 2001 (Vic), and the Health Records and Information Privacy Act 2002 (NSW).

What Is Collected

To provide psychological services, Waystone collects personal and health information including contact details, health history, billing information, and clinical records. Information is collected directly from you or from referring practitioners with your consent.

How It Is Used

Your information is used solely to provide psychological services, process Medicare rebates, communicate with treating practitioners with your consent, and meet legal and ethical obligations.

Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using encrypted, Australian privacy compliant platforms. Full details are provided in the Client Information and Consent Form provided at intake.

Disclosure

Your information will not be shared without your consent except where required by law, necessary to prevent serious risk to you or another person, or required under mandatory reporting obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and make complaints about your personal information. Requests can be directed to katy@waystoneclinicalpsychology.com.au and will be responded to within 30 days.

Website

This website may collect non-identifiable analytics data. No personally identifiable information is collected through browsing.

Complaints

Please contact Katy directly in the first instance at katy@waystoneclinicalpsychology.com.au. If unresolved, you may contact the following bodies:
