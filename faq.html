A Mental Health Treatment Plan (MHTP) is a document prepared by your GP that recommends psychological treatment. It entitles eligible clients to a rebate on up to 10 individual psychology sessions per calendar year. Book a longer appointment with your GP, let them know you would like a referral to see a psychologist, and they will prepare the plan if appropriate.
