Areas of Interest

Katy works with adults experiencing:

  • Trauma, including PTSD and complex trauma
  • Shame, self-worth, and identity
  • Relationship difficulties and attachment patterns
  • Anxiety and worry
  • Depression and low mood
  • Perfectionism, self-criticism, and burnout
  • People-pleasing and difficulty with boundaries
  • Gender identity exploration and WPATH assessment
  • Sexuality and sexual function concerns
  • ADHD
  • Life transitions and major change

Not sure if your concerns are covered? You are always welcome to get in touch – Katy is happy to discuss whether she can help, or point you in the right direction.
