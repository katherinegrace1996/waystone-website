About Medicare Rebates

With a Mental Health Treatment Plan (MHTP) from your GP or psychiatrist, you can access up to 10 rebated sessions per calendar year. The Medicare rebate is processed at the time of payment and typically returned to your nominated bank account within 24 hours.

A new MHTP is required each calendar year. Speak with your GP for more information.

Important to know

Cancellation Policy

Waystone Clinical Psychology has a cancellation policy to respect the time of both client and practitioner. Please provide as much notice as possible if you are unable to attend a scheduled appointment.

Notice provided Charge applied
More than 48 hours No charge
Less than 48 hours 50% of session fee ($140.00)
Less than 24 hours / no show 100% of session fee ($280.00)

Payment Methods

Sessions can be paid by credit card or bank transfer. Payment is required at the time of your appointment.

Waystone Clinical Psychology is not currently registered to provide services under NDIS, WorkCover, DVA, or TAC funding. If cost is a barrier, please get in touch and Katy can point you toward services that may be able to assist.
